Dewey's Network RULES

Screen Sharing Policy & Information
1 If you disconnect during a screen check, try to delete files, empty the recycle bin, clear browser history, terminate certain Windows processes, or modify specific game registries before sharing, you will receive a 15-day TempBan from Dewey's Network.
2 Dewey's Network staff reserve the right to start screen sharing at any time to supervise rule compliance. Refusing will result in a 15-day TempBan.
3 All screen shares are recorded by the staff conducting the check. Players can request an admin to review them if desired.
4 Staff will not access private information. If you believe a staff member has viewed your private data, contact an administrator.
5 Staff may use additional programs to identify unauthorized modifications, processes, or software. If in doubt, ask the staff conducting the check.
1. General Rules
1 No Hacked Clients, Ghost Clients, Injection Clients, External Clients, illegal mods, or any client/mod that gives an advantage over other players. Using them results in a 20-day TempBan.
2 No Autoclickers, Macros, or non-manual click methods. Using them will result in a 20-day TempBan. Jitter and drag click are not allowed.
3 Using a Debounce Time below 10ms is strictly prohibited, resulting in a 20-day TempBan.
4 Exploiting any bug is strictly forbidden and must be reported to staff. Failure results in a 3-day TempBan.
5 The /report command is only for reporting players. For other issues, open a Discord ticket. Misuse will be punished.
6 Lag Machines are strictly forbidden. Violations will be punished according to severity and staff discretion.
7 Owning more than one account is prohibited, except for family members (siblings or parents). Violation results in a permanent ban.
8 Environmental griefing is forbidden, including unjustified destruction or building obscene figures. Punishment depends on severity and staff discretion.
2. Chat Rules
1 Bigotry, toxicity, and hate speech including racial or homophobic terms are not tolerated. Circumvention attempts result in a 24-hour TempMute.
2 Insulting or threatening players or staff is forbidden. Circumvention results in a 4-hour TempMute.
3 Advertising other servers or links is forbidden. Violations result in a 1-day TempBan.
4 Doxxing, DDoS, or swatting attempts are forbidden. Threats should be reported to staff. Malicious pranks result in permanent ban.
5 Telling someone to kill themselves is strictly prohibited. Violations result in a 48-hour TempMute.
6 Pretending to be staff or famous people in public chat is forbidden. Violations result in a 10-day TempMute.
7 Using inappropriate nicknames or skins may result in a 24-hour TempBan.
8 Users should write in the server’s main language (English). Violations result in a mute of 3–12 hours.
